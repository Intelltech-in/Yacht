Yacht Maintenance Service | Yacht Repair Service

Yacht Repairs and Maintenance

We specialize in a range of boat maintenance, yacht repairs, yacht mechanic and shipwright services that includes: boat lifting and hard standing, antifouling removal and application, painting, yacht polishing & detailing, custom teakwood work, fibre glass repairs, yacht restorations & conversions, all boat mechanical servicing. Specialist for Volvo Engine Servicing in Dubai. We are conveniently located in Dubai Maritime City, UAE. We provides personal service and exceptional quality and is committed to securing lasting relationships with our customers.
